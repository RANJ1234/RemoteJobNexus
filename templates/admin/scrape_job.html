Scrape Job from URL
How It Works

Enter the URL of a job posting, and our system will attempt to extract job details automatically. The scraped job will be added directly to your job listings.

Tip: For best results, use URLs from major job boards and company career pages.

Example URLs:
  • LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/123456789
  • Indeed: https://www.indeed.com/viewjob?jk=abcdef123456
  • Remote OK: https://remoteok.com/remote-jobs/123456-remote-job
  • Company Sites: https://example.com/careers/job-posting

Data Extraction
Information We Extract
  • Job Title
  • Company Name
  • Location (defaults to Remote)
  • Job Description
  • Requirements
  • Salary Range (when available)
  • Job Type
  • Job Category (Blue/White/Grey-collar)
  • Application URL
Extraction Methods
  1. Primary: Advanced content extraction with Trafilatura
  2. Fallback: HTML parsing with BeautifulSoup
  3. Heuristic analysis for field identification
Important Notes
  • Our scraper respects robots.txt directives and site Terms of Service
  • Not all job sites allow scraping - results may vary
  • You may need to manually edit scraped jobs to add missing details
  • The scraper automatically detects job categories based on keywords
